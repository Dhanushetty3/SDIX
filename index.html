Our Premium Products

We source only the highest grades of Coffee and Arecanut directly from carefully selected plantations.

Indian Coffee Beans

Sourced from the high-altitude regions of southern India, known for their distinct aroma and balanced acidity.

  • Varieties: Arabica (Plantation A, AA), Robusta (Cherry, Parchment)
  • Origin: Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu
  • Taste Profile: Mild, earthy, well-rounded body with subtle spice notes.
  • Processing: Washed / Natural (Unwashed)

Arecanut (Betel Nut)

High-quality Arecanut cultivated in the rich soils of India, processed and sorted with meticulous care.

  • Types: White Arecanut (Chali), Red Arecanut (Boiled)
  • Grades: Whole, Split, Sliced, Boiled (Moti, Vachras, Jamnagar)
  • Quality: Thoroughly dried, free from fungal infection, standardized moisture content.
  • Packaging: Customized jute bags, PP bags (25kg/50kg, as per buyer).

Seamless Global Logistics

At SDIX Pvt Ltd, exporting is a streamlined process. From custom clearance to freight forwarding, our dedicated logistics team ensures your order arrives safely and efficiently.

  • Global Reach: Exporting primarily to the Middle East, Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia.
  • Packaging Standards: Export-worthy packaging conforming to importing country regulations (vacuum-sealed, jute bags, food-grade liners).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies by product. For Coffee, it's typically one 20ft container (approx 19.2 MT for green beans). For smaller trial shipments, please contact our sales team.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we offer complimentary samples to genuine buyers. The buyer only needs to bear the courier charges.

What payment terms do you accept?

We commonly work with 100% Irrevocable LC at sight, or an Advance T/T combined with balance against BL copy.
